Bananas, They're Just Like Us!

Bananas are simply the best fruit in the world. Bananas have slimy, squishy insides encased by easily bruised skin. Bananas come in all shapes, sizes, and colors, but no matter what they look like, they are all bananas and at the end of the day they all come out looking the same in our poo.

Banana Colors, What Do They Mean?

Green bananas taste the most tart. Most people think it's too early to eat a banana that's still green.
Yellow bananas are sweeter than green, but still a little bit tart. Yellow bananas are generally considered to be optimally ripe.
Yellow bananas with brown spots are almost too ripe to eat, but are great in recipes.
Brown and black bananas are too ripe to eat, but they make great banana bread.
